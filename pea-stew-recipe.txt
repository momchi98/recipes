Ingredients:
 - 1 jar of green peas
 - 1 jar of tomato & vegetable sauce
 - 1 tablespoon of flour
 - salt & pepper

Directions:
 1. Add the peas and the tomato&vegetable sauce in a pot.
 2. Stir for 10-15 mins.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 4. Mix the corn flour and 1/2 cup cold water.  Add to the pot.
 5. Stir the peas for 10 more minutes.
